About the Pufferfish genome

Assembly

This site presents version 3 of the Fugu genome, released in July 2002 by the International Fugu Genome Consortium. Fugu rubripes has a very compact genome, with less than 15% consisting of dispersed repetitive sequence, which makes it ideal for gene discovery.

The latest assembly is based on 5.7X coverage of the genome, and includes 329 Mb of the 365 Mb genome. Please refer to the Fugu Project's Webpage for more details of the sequencing effort.

Annotation

Fugu Ensembl is a joint project between the IMCB and the EMBL - EBI to produce and maintain an automatic annotation of the Fugu genome.
