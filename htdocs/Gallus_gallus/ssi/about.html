About the Chicken genome

Assembly

This site presents an annotation of the first draft chicken genome assembly, March 2004 [NIH press release]. The chicken genome sequence was determined by whole genome shotgun at the Genome Sequencing Center at Washington University, St Louis. The analysis of the chicken sequence involves an international group of scientists including individuals from the US, UK, Europe and China.

Warning

This release of G. gallus CGAW contains some sequence that is not specific to chromosome W. A large portion of the sequence assigned to W was done so based on the presence of W-specific repeats. These repeats have now been shown to be not specific to chromosome W. Thus, the only portions of GGAW which should currently be considered specific to W are:

  • Chr W, bases 1 - 195831
  • Chr W, bases 4895452 - 4916845
  • All of Chr W_random
