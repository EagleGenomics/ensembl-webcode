Privacy Policy
  

As part of the registration process, personal data such as name and e-mail address will be required to be submitted; this information is required to submit annotation using this tool. The data collected is to identify and contact the submitter if further information is required about a particular submission.

Submissions can be changed by the owner of the gene submission, or by curators. Curators may wish to contact submitters to discuss particular submission and to resolve any "errors" that may have been made. Any changes made to a submission will be e-mailed to the owner.

If you have any questions please contact Ensembl helpdesk
